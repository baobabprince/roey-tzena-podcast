# Product Guidelines

## Tone & Voice
- **Primary Tone:** Informative & Professional.
- **Description Style:** Clear, concise summaries that focus on the content and technology. Avoid sensationalism; prioritize accuracy and utility for the listener.

## Visual Identity
- **Design Philosophy:** Modern & Minimalist.
- **Artwork Style:** Use clean typography and simple iconography representing audio, connectivity, or technology. Avoid clutter.
- **Color Palette:** Neutral tones (greys, whites, blacks) with a single accent color (e.g., a "tech" blue or green) to signify activity or links.

## User Experience (UX) Priorities
- **Core Principle:** Simplicity and Reliability.
- **Feed Quality:** The RSS feed must always be valid and strictly compliant with podcast standards (Apple Podcasts, Spotify).
- **Performance:** Updates should be predictable. The system's "heartbeat" (scraping schedule) should be transparent to the administrator but invisible to the end listener, who just receives the content.
